Manufacturing questions we want agents to ask

From OTS to advanced CNC routing.

  • Should this be off-the-shelf, CNC machined, additive, or fabricated?
  • Which providers fit this material, tolerance, geometry, and stage?
  • Who can respond quickly with credible quotes?
  • What process constraints should be flagged before RFQ?
  • Can this part be redesigned for easier production?
